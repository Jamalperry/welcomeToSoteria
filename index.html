Welcome to Soteria

This [Jesus] is the Stone which was despised and rejected by you, the builders, but which has become the Head of the corner [the Cornerstone]. [Ps. 118:22.] And there is salvation in and through no one else, for there is no other name under heaven given among men by andin which we must be saved.

Welcome to Soteria. Yes, this page you are now viewing is called Soteria. Think of it as a special place where you frequent to get some special experience that you could find nowhere else. An experience or reality rather, of healing in your body, mind and emotions. A safe haven or fortress of protection from troubles, distressing situations or harm even. A sweet escape from the "noise" of this world and into a dwelling of rest. Let this "place" be your escape from all these and more, as you shall find out that this word holds all these meanings and more. Now, Soteria is the word translated salvation in the bible, and like many other translated words, it holds various meanings which we shall explore on this page. Salvation or Soteria is more than just salvation when one accepts Jesus Christ as Lord and saviour, it is also the word used when talking of being healed, or saved from danger etc. Enjoy!
